About

The Napa ARTwalk supports the creation of original, high-quality and collaborative sculpture from regional artists. Fifteen sculptures are currently exhibiting in downtown Napa, all relating to the theme of Metamorphosis. The current tour will be on view till May 2015. Be sure to vote for your favorite artist and hash tag your pictures!
